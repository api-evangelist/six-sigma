# Six Sigma (six-sigma)
Six Sigma is a data-driven process improvement methodology that uses statistical analysis to reduce defects and variability in business processes, targeting fewer than 3.4 defects per million opportunities (DPMO). Organizations across manufacturing, healthcare, finance, and technology adopt Six Sigma frameworks (DMAIC for improvement, DMADV for design) to drive quality excellence, reduce costs, and improve customer satisfaction.
